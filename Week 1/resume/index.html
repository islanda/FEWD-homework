About Me

I was born in a refugee camp in Thailand after my parents escaped the Killing Fields in Cambodia. With my family, I immigrated to the United States in 1980.

I graduated from the University of Washington with a BA in Interdisciplinary Arts focusing on culture, literature, and the arts. I began my career as a graphic designer, but decided to further my education and graduated from the University of the Arts in Philadelphia with a Masters of Arts in Museum Communication. I have worked with several cultural institutions including the New School of Music, Cambridge Historical Society, Legacies of War, and the Wing Luke Museum of the Asian Pacific American Experience. I am currently the Design Manager for the Boston Lyric Opera.
